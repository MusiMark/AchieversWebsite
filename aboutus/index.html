About Achievers Uganda

Discover our journey, mission, and the passionate team behind Uganda's youth transformation.

Our Story

In 2018, our founder witnessed a recurring gap among talented Ugandan youth: they had dreams but lacked the mindset, emotional tools, and mentorship to turn those dreams into reality. Many struggled with low self-confidence, poor decision-making, and an inability to cope with failure — leading to unemployment and unrealized potential.

That's how Achievers Uganda was born. Starting with small community workshops in Kampala, we have grown into a movement reaching thousands of young people across the country. Our hands-on training in growth mindset, emotional intelligence, and leadership equips youth (10–25) to navigate school, relationships, and future careers with resilience and purpose.

Our Mission

To equip young people in Uganda with the life skills, growth mindset, and emotional intelligence they need to become confident, purpose-driven leaders who thrive in school, work, and life.

Our Vision

A generation of empowered young achievers who break cycles of poverty and limitation, transforming their communities and the nation through mindset-driven success.

The Problem We're Solving

Uganda has one of the youngest populations in the world — over 75% under 30. Yet, many youth leave school without critical soft skills like self-belief, emotional regulation, and goal-setting. Unemployment, peer pressure, and lack of mentorship lead to lost potential. AMP bridges this gap by providing mindset training and real-life skills that formal education often misses.
