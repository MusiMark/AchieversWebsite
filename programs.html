Achievers Mindset Program

Achievers Mindset Program is a program that supports young people between ages 10 -25 to discover themselves and exploit their full potential. The program enables young people to acquire knowledge, skills and practices to help them develop posi- tive mindsets they need to be successful in all aspects of life. The program further helps parents to connect and teach their children how to handle ups and downs of life, to think for themselves and make good decisions, to dream big and work hard to make their dreams happen.

The Achievers mindset program is a time away from our daily schedules as young people to learn, un learn, re- learn, share experiences, connect, network, have fun and enroll new members. More so, the young people's training events addresses both Psycho-socio and socio-economic impacts of COVID 19 disruptions to enable them adjust to the "new normal".

Target

The program targets young people aged 10 - 25 years who want to learn powerful mindset skills that will enable them discover and choose the right path in life.

Goal

  • To empower young people with the mindset and skills they need to succeed in life.
  • To enhance the capacity of parents and guardians to mentor their children.

Scope and Program coverage

The Achievers Mindset Program is designed to cover all young persons in Uganda both in and out-of-school. The program will be implemented in the urban areas i.e. schools, communities, groups, places of worship, among others.

Program Objectives

The achievers Mindset Program has been developed to:

Objective 1

Build the capacity of young people to make appropriate choices in life

Objective 2

Equip young people with scientific information on the relationship between the mind and reality

Objective 3

Empower young people to manage their thoughts and emotions to withstand adversity

Objective 4

Develop abilities of parents and guardians to build and manage emotional connection with their children

Objective 5

Provide life planning education to young people and develop positive attitude towards work among young people.

Expected Program Outcomes

  • Strong-willed young people
  • Resilient young people;
  • Confident and successful young people;
  • Focused and courageous young people; and Responsible adults

Program Activities

Interactive Training Workshops

Hands-on sessions where youth learn practical skills like leadership, communication, entrepreneurship, and digital literacy through group exercises and real-life scenarios.

Guidance & Counseling Circles

Safe spaces (group or one-on-one) where young people can openly discuss challenges, receive emotional support, and learn coping strategies.

Guest Speakers

Learn directly from successful professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders who share real-life experiences and practical insights.

Peer Educator Program

We train passionate young leaders to become peer educators who inspire, guide, and positively influence their fellow youth.

Parent-Youth Engagement Sessions

Bridging the gap between generations through open conversations, shared experiences, and practical parenting and communication strategies.

Competitions & Challenges

Exciting contests including debates, innovation challenges, and talent showcases that build confidence, creativity, and teamwork.

Mentorship & Coaching

Personalized one-on-one and group mentoring sessions to support youth in achieving their personal, academic, and career goals.

Career Guidance & Development

Helping youth explore career paths, develop job-ready skills, and prepare for future opportunities with confidence.
